About Us

ArtHub was incorporated in 2018 by Vansh Jain. It aims to connect art lovers to artists from all around the globe. By providing a platform for people to share their work, Vansh has helped promote traditional artists and local businesses.

In the few years since its inception, ArtHub has sold over a thousand artworks to customers in India, China and USA. It has over fifty artists from all around the world in its catalogue. ArtHub was featured in numerous magazines and bestowed with awards for its contributions in encouraging fine arts.

ArtHub team is very proud of the work they have done and invites you to browse our collection online. Visit the Gallery section of the website to see the range of artworks currently for sale.
